Warranty Coverage

Clear Warranty Terms

Warranty coverage is handled in writing so expectations are clear before and after the job.

What the Warranty Covers

Warranty coverage applies only to the transmission or drivetrain components serviced or rebuilt by Integrity Transmission & Drivetrain, for the warranty term listed on the completed warranty agreement.

Coverage is limited to defects in workmanship or installed components directly related to the work performed. Warranty coverage is non-transferable and applies only to the listed customer and vehicle.

Warranty Determination

A diagnostic inspection may be required before any warranty decision is made. If the issue is unrelated to the original work, diagnostic or labor charges may apply.

Important Exclusions

  • Abuse, racing, overheating, or lack of maintenance
  • Improper installation or incorrect fluid use
  • External component failure, including cooling systems or electronics
  • Driveshaft, differential, tuning, or vehicle-related issues
  • Contamination, neglect, or continued operation after a known problem
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Towing, downtime, loss of use, or related expenses

Warranty Claim Requirements

Warranty work must be performed by Integrity Transmission & Drivetrain.

The original warranty document should be presented at the time of any claim. If unavailable, shop records must verify coverage before warranty work is considered.
