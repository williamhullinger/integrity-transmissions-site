How Service Works

A better repair starts with the right information.

Transmission and drivetrain symptoms can be misleading. Slipping, no movement, hard shifts, noise, overheating, vibration, leaks, and delayed engagement may point to different causes depending on the vehicle and unit.

The process starts with information, moves into inspection when needed, and then turns into a clear repair direction before major decisions are made.

Why The Details Matter

A transmission repair is only as strong as the decisions behind it.

A fresh rebuild can fail early if the converter is contaminated, the cooler is restricted, hard parts are damaged, electronics are ignored, or the build level does not match the way the vehicle is used.

That is why service recommendations are based on the unit, symptoms, inspection findings, supporting systems, and customer goals — not just the cheapest possible parts list.

Converter condition matters A bad converter can contaminate or damage a fresh rebuild.
Heat and fluid matter Cooling problems and dirty fluid can shorten the life of any repair.
Vehicle use matters Towing, larger tires, work use, and added power can change the right build direction.
Hard parts matter Worn drums, pumps, shafts, planets, sprags, and valve bodies can change the final quote.

Service Questions

Common questions before scheduling work.

Do you rebuild every transmission?

No. Work is limited to supported transmissions and drivetrain units so the repair can be handled properly and expectations stay clear.

Do you offer bench builds?

Yes. Bench builds are available for supported units already removed from the vehicle. Send the unit details before dropping anything off.

Can diagnostics avoid a rebuild?

Sometimes. Not every symptom means the transmission is internally failed. Fluid condition, electronics, leaks, external components, and drivetrain problems may need to be reviewed.

Do you work on transfer cases and differentials?

Yes. Select transfer cases, front IFS differentials, rear differentials, and related drivetrain work are accepted depending on application and condition.
