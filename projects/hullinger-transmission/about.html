About Integrity Transmission & Drivetrain

Built on honesty, precision, and doing the job right the first time.

A Different Kind of Transmission Shop

Integrity Transmission & Drivetrain is a home-based, appointment-only shop serving Springfield, Ozark, and surrounding Missouri areas. This setup allows for a level of attention to detail and quality control that most high-volume shops simply can’t match.

Instead of rushing jobs through, every transmission is carefully torn down, inspected, rebuilt, and verified with precision. No shortcuts. No guesswork.

What That Means For You

  • Direct communication with the person building your transmission
  • No upsells or surprise charges
  • Work done with long-term reliability in mind
  • Clear expectations from start to finish

Licensed & Insured

Integrity Transmission & Drivetrain operates as a registered LLC and is fully insured. You’re not dealing with a backyard hobby — this is a professional business built to last.

Focused Work Only

Not every job is taken. Only transmissions and drivetrain work that can be done properly and confidently are accepted — ensuring consistent quality.

By Appointment

All work is scheduled. This keeps workflow controlled, prevents rushed jobs, and ensures every build gets the attention it deserves.

Experience & Approach

With years of hands-on mechanical experience and a strong focus on drivetrain systems, every build is approached with a clear goal: reliability first.

This isn’t a “swap parts and send it” shop. Every transmission is built with careful attention to tolerances, wear patterns, and known failure points.

  • Thorough teardown and inspection process
  • Quality internal components and upgrades when needed
  • Focus on common failure points and long-term durability
  • Clean, organized assembly with verified clearances

Serving the Springfield & Ozark Area

Located in the Springfield, Missouri area, Integrity Transmission & Drivetrain works with customers locally and throughout the surrounding region.

Many customers find us through word-of-mouth, Facebook, and online searches for reliable, honest transmission work — not just the closest shop.
