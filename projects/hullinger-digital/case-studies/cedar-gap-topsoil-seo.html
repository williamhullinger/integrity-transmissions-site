Case Study

Local SEO Optimization

Cedar Gap Topsoil — Seymour, Missouri

  • Industry: Agricultural / Landscaping Supply
  • Location: Seymour, Missouri (Ozarks Region)
  • Project Type: Local SEO + Website Optimization
  • Platform: GoDaddy Website Builder

Project Overview

Cedar Gap Topsoil is a locally owned supplier of garden-ready topsoil serving homeowners, landscapers, farmers, and contractors across the Ozarks region of Missouri.

The business had an existing website and Google Business Profile but was not fully optimized to capture local search traffic.

The objective of the project was to strengthen the website and business listing so Google could better understand:

  • what the business sells
  • what the product is used for
  • where the business serves customers

These signals are critical for appearing in local search results.

Initial Assessment

The website provided basic information but had several SEO gaps limiting its visibility.

Key Issues Identified

Limited keyword coverage

The site referenced topsoil but lacked many of the search phrases customers use.

Weak geographic targeting

Nearby towns and service areas were not mentioned.

Minimal product context

Search engines had limited information about how the soil is used.

Low conversion visibility

The phone number and contact options were not prominently positioned.

Google Business under-optimized

The listing existed but lacked strong keyword alignment and geographic relevance.

Research

Search behavior was analyzed to understand how customers typically look for soil suppliers.

Common Local Search Queries

  • bulk topsoil
  • garden soil
  • landscaping soil
  • soil delivery
  • fill dirt
  • lawn seeding soil
  • topsoil near me
  • topsoil Springfield MO
  • bulk soil Ozarks

The website content was updated to reflect these search patterns while maintaining natural language.

Website Improvements

Keyword & Content Optimization

New content was added to improve keyword coverage and search relevance.

The website now clearly describes:

  • the type of soil sold
  • the common uses for the soil
  • the types of projects customers use it for

Examples of use-case keywords added:

  • lawn seeding
  • sod preparation
  • landscaping projects
  • horse arenas
  • baseball fields
  • construction fill
  • property leveling

These additions help search engines connect the site with more search queries.

Geographic SEO Improvements

Local SEO relies heavily on geographic relevance.

The website was updated to reference nearby service areas including:

  • Springfield
  • Nixa
  • Ozark
  • Rogersville
  • Marshfield
  • Mountain Grove
  • Mansfield
  • Ava
  • Hartville
  • Fordland
  • Norwood

This allows the website to better match searches for soil suppliers across the surrounding region.

Contact & Conversion Improvements

A clear call-to-action was introduced to make contacting the business easier.

Updates included:

  • phone number placement at the top of the site
  • call-to-action button linked directly to the phone number

This improves both usability and mobile conversions.

Google Business Profile Optimization

The Google Business listing was updated to support stronger local search signals.

Improvements Included

Keyword-optimized description

The business description was rewritten to include:

  • bulk topsoil
  • garden-ready soil
  • landscaping soil
  • Ozarks region
  • Seymour Missouri

Service area expansion

Nearby towns were added to improve geographic reach.

Category verification

Primary category confirmed as:

Topsoil Supplier

Website integration

The website and Google Business listing were verified and synchronized.

SEO Strategy

The optimization focused on the three primary ranking factors used in Google’s local search algorithm:

Relevance

Improving keyword coverage and product descriptions.

Proximity

Expanding geographic references for nearby towns and service areas.

Prominence

Strengthening business information and online signals across the website and Google Business profile.

Outcome

The business now has a stronger digital foundation for local search visibility.

The website and Google Business profile are better aligned to help customers searching for soil suppliers in the Ozarks region discover the business.

These improvements position the company to appear more frequently for searches such as:

  • topsoil Seymour MO
  • topsoil Springfield MO
  • bulk soil Ozarks
  • landscaping soil near me

Services Provided

  • Local SEO strategy
  • Keyword research
  • Website content optimization
  • Geographic targeting
  • Call-to-action improvements
  • Google Business Profile optimization

Key Takeaway

Many local businesses have websites that exist but are not optimized for how customers actually search.

By aligning the website content and Google Business profile with real search behavior, even small structural improvements can significantly strengthen local search visibility.
